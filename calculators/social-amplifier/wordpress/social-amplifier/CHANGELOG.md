= 1.0.0 =
* Initial release. WordPress port of the Social Amplifier (sosha) Node.js tool.
* Campaign management (organizations, campaigns, content, toolkits) via WordPress REST API.
* Embeddable share widget via [social_amplifier_toolkit id="..."] shortcode.
* OAuth 2.0 integration: Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter with PKCE), TikTok.
* Privacy-conscious analytics: share events, click tracking with hashed IPs, aggregated daily stats.
* Token encryption at rest (AES-256-CBC).
* Admin settings page: organization/campaign overview and platform credential management.
